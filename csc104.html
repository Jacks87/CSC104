Recipe

Have you ever made homemade fabric softener with conditioner before? IT WORKS!
With this fabric softener recipe, you will save tons of money and you can pick whatever fragance you want!


Ingredients

* 3 cups of hot water
* 1.5 cups of White Vinegar
* 1 cup of conditioner (your preference of fragance)
* Container for storage
* Whisk


Directions

* Put 3 cups of hot water into a container.
* Add 1 cup of conditioner.
* Using a whisk, stir the water and conditioner until combined.
* Add in the white vinegar.
* Add about 2 tablespoons to an average size load of laundry (depending on the size of your load).
